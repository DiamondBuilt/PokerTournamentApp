# 🏆 Poker League — Player Guide

Welcome to the league! Here's everything you need to know about how the season works
and how points are scored. **Showing up matters, finishing deep matters more, and one
bad night won't sink your season.**

---

## How a season works

- A season runs across a set of game nights (typically **10–24 events over the year**).
- Every **tournament** and **cash game** you play earns league points automatically.
- The **leaderboard** updates live. The **top 6** players at season's end qualify for the
  **Season Finale**.

---

## 🃏 Tournament points

Every tournament you play, you earn:

> **25 points for playing** + **points for your finish** + **10 bonus** if you make the
> final table (top 9).

| If you finish… | You earn |
|:--------------:|:--------:|
| 🥇 **1st** | **135 pts** |
| 🥈 **2nd** | **110 pts** |
| 🥉 **3rd** | **91 pts** |
| 4th | 77 pts |
| 5th | 67 pts |
| 6th | 59 pts |
| 7th | 53 pts |
| 8th | 48 pts |
| 9th | 45 pts |
| 10th | 33 pts |
| 11th + | ~31 and down |

**Just by sitting down you bank a guaranteed 25 points**, plus more for your finish.
Winning is worth more than 4× busting early — but every entry keeps you climbing.

---

## 💵 Cash game points

Every cash session you play, you earn:

> **10 points for attending** + **a bonus for how well you ran** (your profit per hour,
> when you finish ahead).

A losing night still earns the 10 attendance points. Win big and play efficiently, and
the bonus stacks up.

---

## ⚠️ The catch: missing games

For **every league event you skip, you lose 5 points**. Nothing dramatic — but over a
season, the players who keep showing up pull ahead. Consistency wins leagues.

---

## 🥇 The leaderboard

Players are ranked by total points. If two players tie, we break it by:

1. **Most events played** (reward the regulars)
2. **Most tournament wins**
3. **Highest net profit**

The **top 6** earn a seat (and a perk) in the **Season Finale**.

---

## 📺 Following along

- The **leaderboard** is on the **Seasons** screen and the home dashboard.
- During a live game, ask the host to open the **Spectator View** on the TV — it shows
  the clock, blinds, who's left, and the current top 3.
- Standings updates get shared in the group chat after each night.

---

### TL;DR
**Play often. Go deep. Win when you can.** Showing up guarantees points, deep finishes
multiply them, skipping nights costs you, and the top 6 make the finale. Good luck! ♠♥♣♦
